Join the Youth Leadership Program

Be a part of the largest community of youth changemakers. Get your voice heard. Lead the change for an impact. Channelize dreams and steer young minds with YOUTH LEADERSHIP to create an impact by taking the road less travelled.

What is it?

YLP exists for the youth to act in unison with an aim to create an impact by addressing social, political, and environmental issues. It makes them; the youth realize the significance of a youth-led nation. To answer the ‘What’ behind this program’s existence would be to usher the youth to ‘Think of It’ i.e. the ongoing issues pertaining to multifarious spheres, morality, ideals etc.

Why should you join?

The Youth Leadership Program is about equipping you with the skill set required to be a leader while ensuring your growth. You will get a chance to collaborate with the young leaders of our nation. We believe that you are capable of being mature and understanding and that leadership doesn’t necessarily have much to do with age.

Takeaways

In order to lead, you must learn and gain experience in a viable set-up to meet the dynamic changes.From top officials to the best intellects, there’s an abundant potpourri of knowledge waiting to be unraveled. We complete the leaders of tomorrow by compelling them to ‘THINK OF IT’.
